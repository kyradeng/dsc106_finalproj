Duration of Stay

The length of hospital stay varies depending on the type of surgery performed.

Major surgeries like open-heart surgery and organ transplants have longer hospital stays, while minimally invasive procedures tend to have shorter stays.

Average stay duration:

  • Cardiac Surgery: 7-10 days
  • GI Surgeries: 5-8 days
  • Orthopedic Surgeries: 3-6 days
  • Minimally Invasive Surgeries: 1-3 days
