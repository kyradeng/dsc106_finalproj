Admission

Surgical patients tend to be middle-aged on average.

Men are more common to undergo surgery past their 60s. Women are more common to undergo surgery between the ages of 30-60.

Diagnoses

Each dot represents 5 patients that underwent some type of surgery.

Blue dots represent Gastrointestinal (GI) Cancers such as colon cancer, gastric cancer, pancreatic cancer, etc.

Red dots represent Non-GI conditions such as lung cancer, hernias, and breast cancer.

Duration of Stay

How long do patients typically stay in the hospital after surgery?

Patients undergoing major surgeries such as **gastrectomy** or **pancreatectomy** tend to have longer hospital stays, while minor surgeries like **cholecystectomy** result in shorter stays.
